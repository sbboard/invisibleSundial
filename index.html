about us

The Invisible Sundial is the brainchild of composer and multi-instrumentalist Austin Amberg. With the help of some friends, together they create and performs music that spans a wide variety of genres, while shaping his own sound by using unusual instruments and re-contextualizing familiar sounds in musical ways.
The music blurs the timeline between the intricacy of 70s progressive rock, the textural soundscapes of 90s Shoegaze and the cinematic melodies of modern post-rock - with a touch of early electronica.
